    • Research

    Currently my research is mainly focused on applications of,
    1) Comparison, evaluation, and development of force fields for simulations of intrinsically disordered proteins.
    2) Graph-convolutional neural networks in prediction of drug-target binding affinity.
    3) Characterization of self-assembly kinetics of Aβ peptides and its heterogenous interactions with glycosaminoglycans.
    4) Development of artificially modified peptides to inhibit the aggregation of intrinsically disordered proteins.
    5) Protein adsorption, structure and function at liquid interfaces.
